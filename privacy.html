Summary

HumToNotes is designed to work without creating an account. The app needs microphone access to record audio so it can detect pitch and produce note results.

Data we process

  • Audio recordings (microphone): used to detect pitch and map notes.
  • Device/app diagnostics (optional): may be included in a support email if you choose to send one.
