Troubleshooting

  • Microphone permission: Settings → Privacy & Security → Microphone.
  • Background noise: record in a quiet room and hum close to the mic.
  • Steady pitch: avoid heavy vibrato; hum one melody line at a time.
  • Restart: force-close the app and re-open it.
  • Device volume: if monitoring/feedback is enabled, reduce output volume.

Tip: short recordings often produce clearer note results than long takes.
